# freshfold-addis
FreshFold Addis offers professional laundry services in Addis Ababa, including wash & fold, dry cleaning, ironing, and convenient pickup & delivery. It is fast, reliable, and affordable. We care for your clothes with premium quality and same-day service options, making laundry effortless for busy lifestyles.

- Wash & Fold
- Dry Cleaning
- Ironing
- Pickup & Delivery
- Fast, Reliable, and Affordable Service
- Premium Fabric Care
- Same-Day Service Available
